# Phishing Response Playbook

## Goal
Confirm phishing, assess impact, contain, eradicate, and prevent recurrence.

## Triage
- Identify reported email, sender, subject, links/attachments
- Determine if credentials were entered or attachments opened

## Investigation
- Review email headers and link destinations
- Check authentication logs for suspicious logins
- Look for MFA prompts, impossible travel, new devices

## Response
- Reset credentials, revoke sessions/tokens
- Block sender/domain/URL in email gateway/DNS
- Notify impacted users and stakeholders

## Lessons Learned
- Update awareness training and mail filtering rules
